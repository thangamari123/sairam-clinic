About SAIRAM Dental Clinic

Your Journey to Confidence Begins Here!

Sairam dental care located in Dharmapuri, with Leading Dentist In Oddapatti, is the Dental Hospital with exclusive root canal, Braces & Invisalign center. Led by renowned Dr. Raghuraaman (Periodontist) and Dr. Nivetha Raghuraaman (Orthodontist) Sairam dental care offers gum treatment with expertise in laser, root canal treatment and digital smile correction with advanced technology aim to focus on aesthetics, comfort and precision. The clinic also specializes in Invisalign treatment, digital smile makeover, laser gum care, dental implants, root canal therapy, extractions and full-mouth rehabilitation blending compassionate care and clinical excellence.

FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our dental treatments and services.

Dental implant surgery is performed under local anaesthesia, so you won't feel pain during the procedure. Some mild soreness and swelling after the anaesthesia wears off is normal and easily managed with over-the-counter pain relievers. Most patients return to normal activities within 1–2 days.

Most root canal treatments at our clinic are completed in a single visit lasting 60–90 minutes. Complex cases with multiple canals may require 2 visits. We use advanced rotary instruments and digital imaging for faster, more precise treatment — no prolonged discomfort.

We recommend a dental check-up and professional cleaning every 6 months for most patients. Those with gum disease, a history of cavities, or other risk factors may need more frequent visits — typically every 3–4 months. Regular visits help catch problems early before they become costly.
